Questions

Frequently asked

Is the short link permanent? +
Yes, unless you set an expiration date or one-time-use option yourself.
Do I need an account? +
No — links you create are remembered by your browser session. Sign up to keep them permanently and access them from any device.
Do QR codes expire? +
No — a QR code just encodes your short URL, so it stays valid as long as the link does.
What happens after the visitor limit is hit? +
Once the configured interval is reached, the next visitor is routed to whichever redirect URL the admin has set, chosen randomly or in sequence.
